WELCOME to YoungLeaders.World.

We are an international non-profit focused on helping youth get clear on the leadership skills needed to create a better future. We connect and teach through on-line learning, conferences, talks, and annual summits of student leaders. We invite you to connect with us the way that works best for you. Together, we can then get to work on creating an inspiring way forward for you and your community.

For Young Leaders,
By Young Leaders

We collaborate with young people around the world to help them discover life leadership. YL.W is dedicated to helping young people, ages 13-21, understand how to develop a leader mindset. How to think like a leader, make decisions like a leader and articulate your goals. We believe leadership is a mindset, not a status.
