Volunteer Positions

The following are volunteer opportunities that are available with organizations that we are aligned with:

YL.W Student Internship

Youngleaders.World opens up internship opportunities to students in high school in the Edmonton Area. If you have a desire to learn more about leadership, be mentored by some of the leading voices in youth leadership in Canada and have a good work ethic, please apply here.

Intern opportunities for the 2020-21 school year are now closed. Next intake is April 2021.

"YoungLeaders.World showed me what it is like to be working along side an incredibly passionate group of people that has a very hopeful mission for the greater good. YLW has taught me skills that will carry me through all aspects of my life, and help me achieve all my goals." - Riti Shah

YoungLeaders.World is proud to work with the Peter Lougheed Leadership College on the University of Alberta Campus. All successful YLW interns are recommended to the college for an automatic acceptance. *Students must complete their first year of studies at the University of Alberta.
