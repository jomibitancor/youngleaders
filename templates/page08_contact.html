Frequently Asked Questions

QWhat's the difference between Day 1 and Day 2 at Vision2020?

ADay 1, on April 7th, 2020, is a high-energy, high-impact event that costs $20 to attend and includes keynote speeches, a community organization-led IMPACT zone, live music, reflective work time, and more. Day 1 takes place in Hall D at the Edmonton Expo Centre.
Day 2, on April 8th, 2020, consists of small workshops in an intimate setting with the keynotes from Day 1. Day 2 costs $70, this includes a ticket to Day 1.

QWhen is Vision2020?

AThe summit on April 7th, 2020 (Day 1) is from approximately 9:30am to 2:30pm, and the day 2 workshops on April 8th, 2020 are from 9:30am to 3:00pm - Busses should arrive around 9:00 am for dropoff and 2:40 pm for pickup.

QHow do I get in?

AYou can buy tickets, and even pay online here. Students in grades 8 through 12 are encouraged to approach their teachers to buy tickets for multiple students all at once, and book a bus for dropoff.

QI want to attend on my own, not with my school, how does that work?

AYou're welcome to come as an individual or with a parent or friend. Registration is online, we will mail your ticket to your home.

QCan I pay at the door?

AYes. In order to streamline the registration at the door, we're trying to register and receive payments ahead of time. Funneling 3000 people into an event can be challenging - but we'll take cash, mastercard, or visa at the door (may be subject to transaction fees).

QIs lunch provided?

ANo. Please bring a lunch.

QCan I attend if I am not in Grade 8-12?

AIf you are a University student, a professional, parent, or anyone else above the age of 13, you're welcome to attend. This Summit is for all ages 13 and up.
